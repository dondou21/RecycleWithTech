Our Mission

To foster a sustainable mindset in Kigali by engaging residents in eco-friendly practices through recycling. Our platform not only encourages a habit of thinking green but also provides economic benefits, offering a space for users to buy, sell, or exchange second-hand items. We aim to uplift vulnerable communities by creating job opportunities for women and the underprivileged, ensuring both environmental and social impact.

Our Vision

To inspire a city-wide recycling culture where sustainability is a way of life. We envision Kigali as a hub where environmental consciousness thrives, and every citizen participates in reducing waste while benefiting economically from eco-friendly practices. Through this, we strive to create a more equitable and environmentally responsible society.
